We welcome all poker enthusiasts - whether you are a beginner to the game or Phil Ivey! Join us at our weekly hangouts, general body meetings, freeroll tournaments, and cash games, where the stakes are high, and the atmosphere is electric. Our club isn’t just about winning hands; it's a place to hone your analytical skills and make lifelong connections with peers and alumni passionate about poker and strategic thinking. Dive into specialized workshops, join competitive study groups, and meet industry leaders, who share your love for the game. By joinings PPC, you’ll gain an edge both on and off the table!

Our Mission

PPC is committed to providing an inclusive and competitive landscape for Penn students to building their analytical and decision making skills. We provide opportunities regardless of experience level or risk tolerance.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why attend our GBMs?

    We host lessons, practice games, and discussions about strategies and poker fundamentals.

  • How do I become a general member?

    Attend any of our events to get involved. Join our Listserv and request membership through Penn Clubs!

  • I have never played before?

    Beginners are always welcome, and we offer guidance and teach strategies for those new to the game.

  • I do not want to play with cash?

    Cash games are optional, and we offer plenty of free games and tournaments.

  • How to become a sponsor?

    If interested, please email us at pennpokerclub@gmail.com. We will provide our sponsorship package and benefits at every level!

  • Do we run online games/tourneys?

    Yes, we frequently organize online games and tournaments for members. Feel free to join our Discord to play online.
